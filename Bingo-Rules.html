RULES

Bingo is a Multi-player game of numbers and the objective of the game is to mark 5 sets of rows or columns or diagonal entities

1Each player picks up a number in his turn and all the players have to mark the picked number
2If a player is able to mark all the 5 elements of a row or column or diagonal ends then he can mark a "B" at the bottom
3The game proceeds till a player has marked B I N G O buttons at the bottom and whoever finishes all the 5 sets is considered the winner
